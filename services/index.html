Software Integration

Disconnected systems create friction, data silos, and manual work. I help organizations connect their technology stack so information flows seamlessly.

Whether you need to integrate third-party services, connect internal applications, or build event-driven architectures, I architect solutions that are reliable and maintainable.

What I deliver:

  • Event-driven systems
  • Message queue implementation
  • Service mesh configuration
