About

Independent technology consulting built on expertise, transparency, and genuine partnership.

Aaron Brown, Principal Consultant

I'm a technology consultant based near Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, focused on helping businesses navigate the complex world of software development and operations.

My practice centers on three core areas: building custom software solutions, integrating disparate systems, and implementing DevOps practices that enable teams to deliver value faster and more reliably.

What sets independent consulting apart is the direct relationship. You work with me, not a rotating cast of junior consultants. I bring full ownership and accountability to every engagement.

I serve clients ranging from early-stage startups to established enterprises, across industries including healthcare, manufacturing, finance, insurance, retail, and education. This breadth gives me perspective that narrow specialists can't offer.
